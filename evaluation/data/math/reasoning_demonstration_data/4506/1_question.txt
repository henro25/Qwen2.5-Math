Problem: A parallelogram is generated by the vectors $\begin{pmatrix} 2 \\ 1\\ 1 \end{pmatrix}$ and $\begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ - 1 \end{pmatrix}.$

[asy]
unitsize(0.4 cm);

pair A, B, C, D;

A = (0,0);
B = (7,2);
C = (1,3);
D = B + C;

draw(A--B,Arrow(6));
draw(A--C,Arrow(6));
draw(B--D--C);
draw(B--C,dashed);
draw(A--D,dashed);
[/asy]

If $\theta$ is the angle between the diagonals, then find $\cos \theta.$
