Answer: **Step 1:** Determine the value in US dollars.

- 1 US dollar = 7 Namibian dollars.
- Therefore, to convert Namibian dollars to US dollars:  
  \[
  \text{US dollars} = \frac{105 \text{ Namibian dollars}}{7} = 15 \text{ US dollars}.
  \]

**Step 2:** Convert US dollars to Chinese yuan.

- 1 US dollar = 6 Chinese yuan.
- Thus,  
  \[
  15 \text{ US dollars} \times 6 \text{ Chinese yuan/US dollar} = 90 \text{ Chinese yuan}.
  \]

**Step 3:** Alternatively, use direct conversion between Namibian dollars and Chinese yuan.

- Given that 7 Namibian dollars = 6 Chinese yuan, the conversion factor is:  
  \[
  \frac{6 \text{ Chinese yuan}}{7 \text{ Namibian dollars}}.
  \]
- Therefore,  
  \[
  105 \text{ Namibian dollars} \times \frac{6}{7} = 90 \text{ Chinese yuan}.
  \]

**Answer:** The painting costs 90 Chinese yuan.
