Problem: Let $a,$ $b,$ $c,$ $d$ be real numbers, none of which are equal to $-1,$ and let $\omega$ be a complex number such that $\omega^3 = 1$ and $\omega \neq 1.$  If
\[\frac{1}{a + \omega} + \frac{1}{b + \omega} + \frac{1}{c + \omega} + \frac{1}{d + \omega} = \frac{2}{\omega},\]then find
\[\frac{1}{a + 1} + \frac{1}{b + 1} + \frac{1}{c +1} + \frac{1}{d + 1}.\]
