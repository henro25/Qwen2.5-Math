Problem: Let $\mathbf{v}_0$ be a vector.  The vector $\mathbf{v}_0$ is projected onto $\begin{pmatrix} 3 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix},$ resulting in the vector $\mathbf{v}_1.$  The vector $\mathbf{v}_1$ is then projected onto $\begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix},$ resulting in the vector $\mathbf{v}_2.$  Find the matrix that takes $\mathbf{v}_0$ to $\mathbf{v}_2.$
