Problem: There exists a scalar $k$ such that for any vectors $\mathbf{a},$ $\mathbf{b},$ and $\mathbf{c}$ such that $\mathbf{a} + \mathbf{b} + \mathbf{c} = \mathbf{0},$ the equation
\[k (\mathbf{b} \times \mathbf{a}) + \mathbf{b} \times \mathbf{c} + \mathbf{c} \times \mathbf{a} = \mathbf{0}\]holds.  Find $k.$
