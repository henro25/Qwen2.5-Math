Answer: Given that \( wz = 12 - 8i \) and \( |w| = \sqrt{13} \), we need to find \( |z| \).

first, we calculate the magnitude of \( wz \):
\[
|wz| = \sqrt{12^2 + (-8)^2} = \sqrt{144 + 64} = \sqrt{208} = 4\sqrt{13}
\]

using the property of magnitudes of complex numbers, we have:
\[
|wz| = |w| \times |z|
\]

substituting the known values:
\[
4\sqrt{13} = \sqrt{13} \times |z|
\]

solving for \( |z| \):
\[
|z| = \frac{4\sqrt{13}}{\sqrt{13}} = 4
\]

thus, the magnitude of \( z \) is:
\[
\boxed{4}
