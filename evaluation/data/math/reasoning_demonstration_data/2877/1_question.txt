Problem: The greatest integer function, $\lfloor x\rfloor$, denotes the largest integer less than or equal to $x$. For example, $\lfloor3.5\rfloor=3$, $\lfloor\pi\rfloor=3$ and $\lfloor -\pi\rfloor=-4$. Find the sum of the three smallest positive solutions to $x-\lfloor x\rfloor=\frac1{\lfloor x\rfloor}.$ Express your answer as a mixed number.
