Answer: To compute \(54 \times 46\) in your head, you can use the distributive property.

1. Break down 46 into 40 and 6:
   \[
   54 \times 46 = 54 \times (40 + 6)
   \]

2. Calculate \(54 \times 40\):
   \[
   54 \times 40 = 54 \times 4 \times 10 = 216 \times 10 = 2160
   \]

3. Calculate \(54 \times 6\):
   \[
   54 \times 6 = (50 \times 6) + (4 \times 6) = 300 + 24 = 324
   \]

4. Add the two results together:
   \[
   2160 + 324 = 2484
   \]

Therefore, \(54 \times 46 = 2484\).
