Problem: The line with equation $y = x$ is an axis of symmetry of the curve with equation
\[y = \frac{px + q}{rx + s},\]where $p,$ $q,$ $r,$ $s$ are all nonzero.  Which of the following statements must hold?

(A) $p + q = 0$

(B) $p + r = 0$

(C) $p + s = 0$

(D) $q + r = 0$

(E) $q + s = 0$

(F) $r + s = 0$
