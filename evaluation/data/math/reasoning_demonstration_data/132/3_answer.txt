Answer: To determine the sum of the prime numbers less than 10:

1. **Definition of a prime number:** A prime number is a number greater than 1 that has no positive divisors other than 1 and itself.

2. **List of prime numbers less than 10:**
   - 2 (prime)
   - 3 (prime)
   - 4 (not prime, divisible by 2)
   - 5 (prime)
   - 6 (not prime, divisible by 2 and 3)
   - 7 (prime)
   - 8 (not prime, divisible by 2)
   - 9 (not prime, divisible by 3)

   Therefore, the prime numbers less than 10 are: 2, 3, 5, and 7.

3. **Sum of these prime numbers:**
   - 2 + 3 + 5 + 7 = 17

**Answer:** The sum of the prime numbers less than 10 is 17.
