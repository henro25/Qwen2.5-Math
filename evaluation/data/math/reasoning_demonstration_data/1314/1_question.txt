Problem: Let $ a$, $ b$, $ c$, $ x$, $ y$, and $ z$ be real numbers that satisfy the three equations
\begin{align*}
  13x +  by +  cz &= 0 \\
   ax + 23y +  cz &= 0 \\
   ax +  by + 42z &= 0.
\end{align*}Suppose that $ a \ne 13$ and $ x \ne 0$.  What is the value of
\[ \frac{a}{a - 13} + \frac{b}{b - 23} + \frac{c}{c - 42} \, ?\]
