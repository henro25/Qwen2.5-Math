   \[

   \[
   4^4 = 4 \times 4 \times 4 \times 4 = 256
   \]

   \[
   8^2 = 8 \times 8 = 64
   \]

4. **Substitute these values back into the equation:**
   \[
   L = \frac{25 \times 256}{64}
   \]

5. **Multiply 25 by 256:**
   \[
   25 \times 256 = 6,400
   \]

6. **Divide 6,400 by 64:**
   \[
   \frac{6,400}{64} = 100
   \]
