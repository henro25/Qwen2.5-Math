Answer: To solve the problem, we first calculate each part separately:

1. **First expression:**
   \[
   \frac{6^2 + 6^2}{6}
   \]
   - \(6^2 = 36\)
   - \(36 + 36 = 72\)
   - \(72 \div 6 = 12\)

2. **Second expression:**
   \[
   \frac{6^2 \times 6^2}{6}
   \]
   - \(6^2 = 36\)
   - \(36 \times 36 = 1296\)
   - \(1296 \div 6 = 216\)

Finally, we find the positive difference between the two results:
\[
216 - 12 = 204
\]

**Answer:** 204
