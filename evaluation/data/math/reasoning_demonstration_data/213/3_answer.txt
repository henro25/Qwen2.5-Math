Answer: Given the areas of three faces of a right rectangular prism as 48, 49, and 50 square units, we need to find the volume of the prism.

let the dimensions of the prism be \( l \), \( w \), and \( h \). the areas of the faces are:
\[
l \times w = 48, \quad l \times h = 49, \quad w \times h = 50
\]

to find the volume \( v = l \times w \times h \), we multiply the three equations:
\[
(l \times w) \times (l \times h) \times (w \times h) = 48 \times 49 \times 50
\]
\[
(l \times w \times h)^2 = 48 \times 49 \times 50
\]
\[
(l \times w \times h)^2 = 117600
\]
\[
l \times w \times h = \sqrt{117600}
\]

simplifying the square root:
\[
\sqrt{117600} = \sqrt{1176 \times 100} = \sqrt{1176} \times 10
\]
\[
1176 = 49 \times 24 \quad \text{and} \quad \sqrt{1176} = \sqrt{49 \times 24} = 7 \sqrt{24}
\]
\[
\sqrt{24} = 2 \sqrt{6} \quad \text{so} \quad \sqrt{1176} = 7 \times 2 \sqrt{6} = 14 \sqrt{6}
\]
\[
l \times w \times h = 10 \times 14 \sqrt{6} = 140 \sqrt{6}
\]

approximating \( \sqrt{6} \approx 2.449 \):
\[
v = 140 \times 2.449 \approx 342.86
\]

rounding to the nearest whole number, the volume is:
\[
\boxed{343}
\]
