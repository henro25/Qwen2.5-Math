Answer: To simplify \(\sqrt{7 \cdot 2} \cdot \sqrt{2^3 \cdot 7^3}\), we can follow these steps:

1. combine the square roots:
   \[
   \sqrt{7 \cdot 2} \cdot \sqrt{2^3 \cdot 7^3} = \sqrt{(7 \cdot 2) \cdot (2^3 \cdot 7^3)}
   \]

2. simplify the expression inside the square root:
   \[
   (7 \cdot 2) \cdot (2^3 \cdot 7^3) = 7^1 \cdot 7^3 \cdot 2^1 \cdot 2^3 = 7^{1+3} \cdot 2^{1+3} = 7^4 \cdot 2^4
   \]

3. recognize that \(7^4 \cdot 2^4\) can be written as \((7 \cdot 2)^4\):
   \[
   7^4 \cdot 2^4 = (7 \cdot 2)^4 = 14^4
   \]

4. take the square root of \(14^4\):
   \[
   \sqrt{14^4} = 14^{4/2} = 14^2 = 196
   \]

thus, the simplified form is \(\boxed{196}\).
