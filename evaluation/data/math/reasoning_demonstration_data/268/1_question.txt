\[\begin{pmatrix} 3 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} 18 & 12 \\ -20 & -13 \end{pmatrix}.\]Find the smallest possible value of $a + b + c + d.$
