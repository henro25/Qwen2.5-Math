Problem: In right triangle $ABC$, $AB=9$, $BC=13$, and $\angle B = 90^\circ$.  Points $D$ and $E$ are midpoints of $\overline{AB}$ and $\overline{AC}$ respectively; $\overline{CD}$ and $\overline{BE}$ intersect at point $X$.  Compute the ratio of the area of quadrilateral $AEXD$ to the area of triangle $BXC$.
