Answer: The value of $\sqrt{3^3 + 3^3 + 3^3}$ is 9.

**Step-by-Step Explanation:**

1. **Calculate $3^3$:**
   \[
   3^3 = 3 \times 3 \times 3 = 27
   \]

2. **Sum the three $3^3$ terms:**
   \[
   3^3 + 3^3 + 3^3 = 27 + 27 + 27 = 81
   \]

3. **Find the square root of 81:**
   \[
   \sqrt{81} = 9
   \]

**Answer:** 9
