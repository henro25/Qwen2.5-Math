Problem: The positive integers $A,$ $B,$ $A-B,$ and $A+B$ are all prime numbers. The sum of these four primes is

$\bullet$ A. even

$\bullet$ B. divisible by $3$

$\bullet$ C. divisible by $5$

$\bullet$ D. divisible by $7$

$\bullet$ E. prime

Express your answer using a letter, as A, B, C, D, or E.
