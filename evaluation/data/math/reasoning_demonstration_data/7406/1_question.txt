Problem: The vectors $\mathbf{a} = \begin{pmatrix} 3 \\ 1 \\ -2 \end{pmatrix}$ and $\mathbf{b} = \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 2 \\ -1 \end{pmatrix}.$  There exist scalars $p,$ $q,$ and $r$ such that
\[\begin{pmatrix} 4 \\ 1 \\ -4 \end{pmatrix} = p \mathbf{a} + q \mathbf{b} + r (\mathbf{a} \times \mathbf{b}).\]Find $r.$
