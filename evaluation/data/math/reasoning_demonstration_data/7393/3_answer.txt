Answer: To find the arithmetic mean of 14, 22, and 36:

1. **Calculate the sum of the numbers:**
   \[
   14 + 22 + 36 = 72
   \]

2. **Count the number of values:**
   There are 3 values.

3. **Divide the sum by the number of values:**
   \[
   \frac{72}{3} = 24
   \]

**Answer:** The arithmetic mean of 14, 22, and 36 is 24.
